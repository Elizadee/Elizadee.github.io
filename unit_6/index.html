What We Do

ScapeGoat connects homeowners and businesses to a network of qualified design professionals in providing their landscaping needs and to help bring their vision to life. ScapeGoat makes venturing into the process of planning your next project easier. Here are a few reasons to join:

  • Find and connect with a network of reputable design professionals.
  • Get and compare quotes through a list of experts in your area.
  • Engage and hire a professional that meets your design criteria and budget needs with confidence.
  • Find other service technicians for landscape related work.

How it Works

  1. Tell us about your project. Build a summary description of your project needs in our form. (Give us some details.)
  2. Submit and launch a search bid for design professionals in your area.
  3. Compare quotes. Engage and communicate with leading matches.
  4. Hire the expert that best fits your needs.

Got a project in mind?

No project is too big or small, whether a home residence or a commercial setting. Our network conists of highly skilled design professionals who have many years of experience planning projects of all sizes.
